Rabbit sprite art — credits & licence
======================================

The rabbit's artwork in this folder (idle/walk/run/fall/drag/sleep/eat/pet/
climb/land PNGs) is derived from the "Light Brown Bunny" Shimeji image set.
It was resized/cropped and repacked into sprite sheets for Pocket Pet, but the
artwork itself is NOT original to this project.

Artist:      FluffyFoxOfFate
             https://www.deviantart.com/fluffyfoxoffate
             Support: https://ko-fi.com/fluffyfoxoffate

Framework:   Shimeji-ee (Shimeji English Enhanced) by Kilkakon
             https://kilkakon.com/shimeji
             Original Shimeji by Yuki Yamada / Group Finity
             http://www.group-finity.com/Shimeji/

Permission (from the Shimeji-ee pack's licence.txt, by Kilkakon):
  "You are welcome to use this work in your own projects if you credit
   Kilkakon and the original people who worked on this. A link to
   kilkakon.com would also be nice."

Accordingly, credit goes to FluffyFoxOfFate (art) and Kilkakon / Group Finity
(framework + original Shimeji). See CREDITS.md in the project root for the full
notice and the Shimeji-ee BSD licence text.
